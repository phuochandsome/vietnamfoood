Hạ Long Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in northern Vietnam, famous for its emerald water and thousands of limestone islands. Things to do: Tailor-made clothes, cooking classes, boat rides, cycling to countryside villages.

Hội An is a UNESCO-listed ancient town in central Vietnam known for its lantern-lit streets, historic architecture, and riverside charm.

Hà Nội is a historic and vibrant city known for its centuries-old architecture, lively street culture, and blend of French, Chinese, and Vietnamese influences.
